# AlPFA Mobile app

> Empowering latino profesionals, association dedicated for foresting career growth and much more!


This is the experimental github page of the incredible and groundbreaking soon to be made, ALPFA's app!!

## Vision

This ALPFA mobile app will be created with a focus on Rutgers Newark as of currently. It will be a **central hub** for our members and anybody who would like to join. 

Not matter who you are; whether a student, professional, or an alien, we will try to bombard you with resources and opportunities in the community so you don't miss out how awesome the city of Newark is!
